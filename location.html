Location and Map

Kera apartments is a beach-side hotel, in the northern coast of Chania, built within 7,000 m² of maintained gardens in the region of Apokoronas, just 20 km away from the town of Chania.
Its privileged position, beside the sea and away from the traffic, combines absolute relaxation and tranquillity, while at the same time, offers the opportunity to taste traditional meals, and take part in many other activities, at the nearby villages namely Kalyves and Almyrida, which you can reach either on foot, because of the small distance or by bicycle or car. For any further information about traveling around, we are always willing to help you.


View Kera Apartments in a larger map

All the apartments offer views to its surrounding countryside, Souda bay, and the open sea, which you can reach, just walking down some stairs. To the South you can enjoy the magnificent White Mountains, including Crete's second highest peak. A quiet and friendly environment, providing a holiday to remember in one of the most beautiful regions of Crete.

The image at right shows a map of the entire area as well as a simplified plan for your easy access to Kera. Kera apartments are located in a distance of 15 km from the port of Souda and 25 km from the airport.
We can give you all the information needed for your transportation, or even arrange it.
