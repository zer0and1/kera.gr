KERA Apartments consist of 16 separate apartments (5 of 25m²,
9 of 35m² and 2 of 60m²), each one of them including a spacious bedroom and living room, a full-equipped kitchen, a bathroom, and
a balcony with a magnificent view to the sea.
The whole outdoor environment is surrounded with gardens full of flowers, palm trees, vineyards, and fruit-trees.
A cozy shaded terrace with view to Souda bay invites you to relax and enjoy your breakfast.
Although the sea lies only a few meters away, there is also a large, 170m² swimming pool.
KERA Apartments offer friendly service, daily room-cleaning, and
a shaded parking for your vehicles.
